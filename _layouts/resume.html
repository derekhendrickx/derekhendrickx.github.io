Markerless motion capture with the Microsoft Kinect (MIIRE research team)

Developer • January 2013 — May 2013

Microsoft Kinect, a device that embeds color video sensor and a depth sensor, has made its place in the toolbox of 3D animators. The strength of the Kinect is the ability to find the skeleton of the person in front of the sensor, incorporating a quick recognition and requires no training to use. The objective of this project was to generate real-time full 3D motion data in a standard format like Biovision Hierarchy (BVH).
